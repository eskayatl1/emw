Reserve yours!

We fulfill every reservation we receive in the order they are received. Please contact us to find out our current queue and lead time.

  • Reservation fee: $10,000
  • Delivery of donor vehicle to Eddy: 75% of vehicle price
  • Delivery to customer: 25% of vehicle price

Upon receiving your reservation submission, we will send an invoice for the reservation fee. Upon receiving the fee, we will secure your position in our build queue. The reservation fee is, of course, applied toward the total cost of your vehicle and it is completely refundable up until one month prior to your build’s start date.
