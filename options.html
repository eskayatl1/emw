  • Full brake system flush and inspection
  • Replacement of all pads, shoes, rotors, or drums and damaged components

  • Power brakes retained via ultra-quiet electric vacuum pump

Upgraded Steering System

See our warranty information on the electric conversions page. Keep in mind that motors have minimum voltage requirements and to achieve the power ratings listed below, a minimum number of battery modules is required. Ultimately, max power is dictated by the peak output of the battery system. Contact us for more information on pairing a motor with the correct battery system.

Battery

We specialize in integrating and interfacing with Tesla Model S/X battery modules, as they have the highest energy-weight ratio of any affordable battery system. We have worked closely with Prism Motors to develop a drop-in battery management system (BMS) that works directly with Tesla modules. These batteries offer excellent range, longevity, and safety, however in smaller vehicles it may be impossible to package enough modules to obtain required voltages for our motor systems. In such situations, we may advise the use of Chevy Volt modules. These provide an excellent power-weight ratio, meaning we can obtain operating voltages in a smaller battery size, with less range.

Charging

We include a standard Level-2 J1772 charge port in all our vehicles. The following upgrades are available in addition to the standard charger. Vehicles equipped with DC Fast Charging will retain the Level 2 equipment as well.

6.7 kW On-Board Charger (standard)

  • Level 2, compatible with all J1772 EVSE
  • Air cooled, over-temperature protected
  • Computer controlled, active battery management while charging
  • Full charge times of 5 hours to 15 hours, depending on battery

13.4 kW On-Board Charger (+$4000)

  • Level 2, compatible with all J1772 EVSE
  • Air cooled, over-temperature protected
  • Computer controlled, active battery management while charging
  • Full charge times of 2.5 hours to 7.5 hours, depending on battery

50 kW DC Fast Charging (+$4000)

  • Level 3, compatible with CHAdeMO stations
  • Batteries water-cooled while charging
  • Computer controlled, active battery management while charging
  • Full charge times of 1 hour to 2.5 hours, depending on battery selection
